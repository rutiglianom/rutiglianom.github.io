Tess is one of Sararosa’s friends from journalism school and one of the coolest people you’ll ever meet. Now reunited on the West Coast, Sararosa and Isabelle enjoy Zwifting together on their bike trainers, chatting on the phone and reminiscing on their childhood spent doing cartwheels. Isabelle is a materials engineer, rock climber, runner, and hiker. If you see her, ask her about her latest outdoor adventures.

Jeremy works as an accountant in Los Angeles, and in his free time, he enjoys volunteering, playing pickup basketball, and being truly evil in board games.
