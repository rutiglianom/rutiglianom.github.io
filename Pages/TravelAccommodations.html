Travel & Accommodations

Saint Paul, MN

The wedding weekend will be taking place in Sararosa's home state of Minnesota, specifically the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and Saint Paul. Airlines that serve the MSP airport from the West Coast include Delta, Alaska, and Sun Country — among many others. For those traveling from elsewhere, MSP is a Delta hub and there are plenty of other airlines that connect it to other areas of the country.

Holiday Inn St. Paul Downtown

For your convenience, a block of rooms has been reserved at Holiday Inn in Downtown Saint Paul. This is a little over half a mile to the venue. When calling to make your reservation, please mention the Davies Rutigliano wedding in order to receive a discounted rate. Please call (651) 225-1515 to make your reservation. Be sure to book your hotel room by July 28, 2026 in order to receive the discount.

InterContinental Saint Paul Riverfront

This hotel is closest to the venue — a 4 minute walk on the Wabasha Street Bridge — for those looking for the most convenient place to stay.
